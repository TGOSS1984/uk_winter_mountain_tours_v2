Snowdon via Crib Goch


Tackle the famous knife-edge arête of Crib Goch en route to the summit of Yr Wyddfa. Exposed but spectacular, this Grade 1 ridge links the Pyg Track with a thrilling crest and unforgettable views into the cwms below.

From £100 per person

Book This Tour

Tryfan North Face & Bristly Ridge


Scramble the sculpted North Face of Tryfan to its summit monoliths, then cross Bwlch Tryfan to the pinnacled Bristly Ridge. A classic Snowdonia double that delivers hands-on movement and big mountain atmosphere.

From £95 per person

Book This Tour

Y Gribin (Glyderau)


A well-defined ridge rising above Llyn Bochlwyd to the moonscape of the Glyderau. Solid rock steps and a broad crest make this an ideal first Welsh scramble with rewarding summit vistas.

From £85 per person

Book This Tour

Pen yr Ole Wen


A steep pull from the valley leading to expansive views across Ogwen and the Carneddau. A punchy ascent that feels alpine in character from first step to summit ridge.

From £85 per person

Book This Tour

Carnedd Dafydd via Crib Lem Spur


Leave the crowds behind on the airy but amenable Crib Lem Spur. This elegant rib climbs to the broad shoulder of Carnedd Dafydd with sweeping views over Cwm Llafar and the high Carneddau.

From £90 per person

Book This Tour

Cnicht


The shapely “Welsh Matterhorn” rising above the slate country of Croesor. Rocky steps, a fine summit, and big estuary views make this a memorable Moelwynion day.

From £80 per person

Book This Tour

Moel Siabod


A solitary sentinel with panoramic views of the Snowdon horseshoe and the Glyderau. Option to include the Daear Ddu Ridge for satisfying hands-on movement to the summit.

From £85 per person

Book This Tour

Nantlle Ridge


A graceful ridge walk linking a chain of rocky tops. Enjoy sea views to the Llŷn Peninsula and wild skylines back to Eryri’s central peaks on this quieter classic.

From £85 per person

Book This Tour

Cadair Idris


A southern Snowdonia icon above the dark waters of Llyn Cau. The classic circuit via the Minffordd Path gives rocky steps, balcony paths, and a superb summit panorama over Cardigan Bay.

From £90 per person

Book This Tour

Pen y Fan


The high point of the Brecon Beacons: sweeping escarpments, grassy ridges and far-reaching views. A classic circuit via Corn Du completes the skyline.
