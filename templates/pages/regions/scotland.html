An Teallach


A mighty Torridonian sandstone ridge whose serrated crest gives thrilling but manageable scrambling. On a clear day the Fisherfield wilderness and Hebridean seascapes feel close enough to touch.

From £110 per person

Book This Tour

Aonach Eagach


Perhaps the narrowest mainland ridge in Scotland. A committing traverse above Glencoe, weaving over pinnacles with constant exposure, big atmosphere, and views down to the valley floor.

From £105 per person

Book This Tour

Beinn Alligin


A magnificent circuit over the Horns of Alligin (Na Rathanan). Rocky steps, airy edges and constant views over Loch Torridon and across to Liathach make this a perfect Torridon day.

From £95 per person

Book This Tour

Ben Macdui


Journey across the high Cairngorm plateau, skirting dramatic corrie rims and boulderfields to reach the vast summit of Ben Macdui. A day of big skies and arctic-scale scenery.

From £90 per person

Book This Tour

Ben Nevis via CMD Arête


An elegant horseshoe linking Carn Mòr Dearg with Ben Nevis by the celebrated arête. Airy but steady scrambling with unforgettable views of the North Face’s great cliffs.

From £100 per person

Book This Tour

Buachaille Etive Mòr


Climb the classic pyramid of Stob Dearg then follow a fine ridge to Stob na Bròige. Optional easy scrambling and constant views over Rannoch Moor and Glen Etive.

From £85 per person

Book This Tour

Grey Corries


A long, elegant traverse of gleaming quartzite tops, with narrow aretes and sweeping views to the Ben and the Mamores. A satisfying big day on the ridges.

From £95 per person

Book This Tour

Liathach


One of Scotland’s finest ridges. A committing traverse with the famed Am Fasarinen pinnacles (with bypass options), delivering huge Torridon panoramas and serious mountain feel.

From £105 per person

Book This Tour

Ring of Steall


A superb Mamores horseshoe above Steall Falls, including the airy Devil’s Ridge. Constant excitement and expansive views across Lochaber’s highest peaks.

From £90 per person

Book This Tour

Suilven


An unforgettable outing in Assynt: a long approach to a narrow fin of rock with a sensational summit crest. Wild country, big skies and views to the sea.

From £85 per person
